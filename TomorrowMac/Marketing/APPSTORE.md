## Description (Short)
TomorrowMac brings intelligent planning to your menu bar. Anticipate tomorrow's priorities, reflect on today's wins, and coordinate with your partner — all without leaving your workflow.

## Description (Full)
### Plan Ahead, Not in the Dark
TomorrowMac lives in your menu bar, quietly learning your rhythms and surfacing what matters before you need to ask.

### Key Features
- **AI Tomorrow Forecast** — Your day, anticipated. Tasks, events, and priorities surfaced before you open your calendar.
- **Quick Planning** — Jot down what matters for tomorrow without breaking focus.
- **Evening Reflection** — End your day with a simple mood check-in. Build the habit of intentional closure.
- **Partner Sync** — Shared planning with your partner, done right. Coordinate tomorrow together without the back-and-forth.
- **Horizon Design** — A calm, forward-looking interface that feels like sunrise, not spreadsheets.

### Privacy First
Your reflections and plans stay on your Mac. No cloud dependency, no accounts required.
